About

Computational research across electronic structure and spectroscopy

My research focuses on understanding correlated quantum materials by combining first-principles electronic-structure calculations with many-body methods and spectroscopic simulations. I am particularly interested in connecting microscopic theory with experimental observables.

I also explore data-driven strategies, including Bayesian optimization and neural networks, to accelerate parameter searches, improve predictive models, and reveal structure in complex materials data.
